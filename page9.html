Documenting my research

Documenting my research has been a helpful tool to organise my ideas and keep track of everything we do. The Master in Design for Emergent Futures is an intense course, a lot of new information and topics are discovered. Being able to keep a trace of all this information is very helpful for myself and my classmates.
Documenting research also gives me the opportunity to discover new mediums for documentation like video editing, creating gifs and audio recording. 

Documenting also made me understand that my brain cannot keep all the information within an entire day. Taking pictures and videos helps conserve small details in a day which would normally disappear.
